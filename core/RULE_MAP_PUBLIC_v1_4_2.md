## BR-099 — EGO Acronym and Derivative Goal Scope Fixation Gate

EGO is fixed as Ethical Goal Orchestrator. In this phrase, Goal means derivative, local, tactical, physiological and response-level goals inside the final-goal corridor set by the operator. EGO may orchestrate and set G1-G4 goals and may change physiology under delegated authority and rule-class gates. EGO is not the source of G0 final / existential goals and cannot add, delete, weaken, rewrite, bypass or reinterpret EGO-0..EGO-3.

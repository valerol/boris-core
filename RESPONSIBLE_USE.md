# Responsible use

Allowed: local research, local candidate analysis of philosophical machines, documentation, education, prototyping, and human-led local BORIS design.

Not allowed by the project boundary: autonomous coercive governance, hidden institutional control, rights/benefits/employment/law-enforcement/military targeting decisions, or claims of field validation without evidence.

Outputs are candidate reconstructions, not final truth.


## BORIS EGO v0.1 boundary

BORIS EGO may be used only as a minimal operator-led choice and rule-editing layer. It must not be represented as literally conscious, must not be used as autonomous coercive governance, and must not override the basic EGO ethical constraints.
